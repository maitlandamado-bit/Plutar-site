Refund Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

This Refund Policy explains when payments may or may not be refunded for Plutar services and software.

1. Setup Fees

Setup fees cover planning, onboarding, and implementation work. Once setup work has started, setup fees are generally non-refundable.

2. Monthly Services

  • Monthly service charges are billed in advance for each billing period.
  • You may cancel before the next billing date to avoid future charges.
  • Partial refunds for unused time in a billing period are generally not provided unless required by law or agreed in writing.

3. Custom Projects

Custom project refunds, milestones, and cancellation terms may differ and are controlled by your signed proposal or written agreement.

4. Chargebacks

If you have a billing issue, contact Plutar first so we can resolve it quickly. Unresolved chargebacks may result in service suspension while the dispute is reviewed.

5. Exceptions

Refund exceptions may be granted at Plutar's discretion when there is a documented duplicate payment, billing error, or legal requirement.

6. Contact

For refund questions, use Plutar contact form and include your email, plan, and payment date.

This page is a general policy and does not replace signed contracts. If your project includes custom terms, the signed agreement controls.
