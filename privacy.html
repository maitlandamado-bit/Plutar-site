Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Plutar collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you visit the website or use Plutar services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact details you submit (such as name, email, business name, and project notes).
  • Billing and transaction details processed by payment providers.
  • Account and software usage information needed to provide services.
  • Technical data such as browser type, device, and approximate location data.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve services, software features, and support.
  • To communicate about onboarding, billing, and service updates.
  • To monitor performance, prevent abuse, and maintain security.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce agreements.

3. How We Share Information

  • With service providers that support operations (for example hosting, forms, payments, scheduling, and analytics).
  • When required by law, legal process, or to protect rights and safety.
  • During business transitions such as mergers, acquisitions, or restructuring.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Plutar and partner tools may use cookies or similar technologies to keep services functioning, measure performance, and improve user experience.

5. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless longer retention is required by law.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or limitation of personal data processing. You may also request information about how your data is used.

7. Security

Plutar uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Plutar is intended for business users and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates are posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

10. Contact

Questions or privacy requests: Contact Plutar.
